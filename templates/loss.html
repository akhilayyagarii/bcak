Weight Loss Tips

To lose weight, focus on burning more calories than you consume. Tips include:

  • Eat a balanced diet with fewer processed foods
  • Incorporate daily exercise
  • Stay hydrated
  • Get enough sleep to support metabolism

Meal Plan: Morning to Night

  • Breakfast (Non-Veg): Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes, served with a slice of whole-grain toast.
    Breakfast (Veg): Tofu scramble with spinach and tomatoes, served with a slice of whole-grain toast.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: A small apple or a handful of mixed berries.
  • Lunch (Non-Veg): Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and a vinaigrette dressing.
    Lunch (Veg): Grilled tofu or chickpea salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and a vinaigrette dressing.
  • Afternoon Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus.
  • Dinner (Non-Veg): Baked salmon with steamed broccoli and quinoa.
    Dinner (Veg): Grilled paneer or roasted chickpeas with steamed broccoli and quinoa.
  • Evening Snack: A cup of herbal tea and a small handful of almonds.

Tracking your calorie intake can help you stay on target.
