Weight Gain Guide

Your complete guide to healthy weight gain

Build muscle and gain weight healthily by consuming more calories than you burn. Focus on nutrient-dense foods and consistent eating patterns.

Essential Weight Gain Tips

Caloric Surplus

Consume 300-500 calories more than your daily maintenance needs. Track your intake to ensure consistency.

Frequent Meals

Eat 5-6 smaller meals throughout the day instead of 3 large ones. This helps maximize nutrient absorption.

Strength Training

Incorporate resistance exercises to build muscle mass rather than just gaining fat. Focus on compound movements.

Nutrient Dense Foods

Choose foods high in healthy fats, proteins, and complex carbohydrates to support healthy weight gain.
