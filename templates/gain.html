Weight Gain Tips

To gain weight, you need to consume more calories than you burn. Focus on nutrient-dense foods like:

Meal Plan: Morning to Night

  • Breakfast (Non-Veg): Oatmeal with peanut butter, banana, and a glass of whole milk
    Breakfast (Veg): Oatmeal with almond butter, banana, and a glass of almond milk or soy milk
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with granola and honey (Veg: Can opt for plant-based yogurt)
  • Lunch (Non-Veg): Grilled chicken sandwich with avocado, whole grain bread, and a side of quinoa salad
    Lunch (Veg): Grilled tofu or paneer sandwich with avocado, whole grain bread, and a side of quinoa salad
  • Afternoon Snack: A handful of mixed nuts and dried fruits
  • Dinner (Non-Veg): Salmon fillet with sweet potatoes, sautéed spinach, and olive oil drizzle
    Dinner (Veg): Grilled paneer or tempeh with sweet potatoes, sautéed spinach, and olive oil drizzle
  • Evening Snack: Cottage cheese with a drizzle of olive oil and some whole grain crackers (Veg: Can opt for plant-based cheese or tofu spread)

Consistency in consuming more calories is key to weight gain.
